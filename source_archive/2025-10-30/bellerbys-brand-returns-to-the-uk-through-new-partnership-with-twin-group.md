Bellerbys Global
, part of
Study Group
, has partnered with
Twin Group
, a UK-based international education specialist, to deliver the Bellerbys foundation program in the UK.
This partnership marks the return of the 45-year-old Bellerbys brand in the UK, following the 2022
closure
of Bellerbys College due to enrolment declines.
Starting in September 2026, the Bellerbys foundation program, delivered by Twin, will give international students the opportunity to students will build the academic and language skills required for university study either the Twin Eastbourne campus or Twin London’s Greenwich campus.
Upon completion, students can progress to one of more than 50 partner institutions affiliated with Bellerbys Global.
“We’re proud to partner with Bellerbys Global to deliver this renowned programme at our Eastbourne and London campuses,” said Caroline and Jacqui Fox, co-founders of Twin Group.
As the organisation marks its 30th anniversary, the co-founders say the partnership underscores an “ongoing commitment to innovation and opportunity in international education, strengthening our portfolio for the benefit of international students”.
Our collaboration with Twin will once again allow students to experience that same Bellerbys standard in the UK as part of a global network
July Behl, Study Group
Meanwhile, July Behl, chief partnership officer at Study Group, stressed that for over 40 years Bellerbys has represented “academic quality, progression success and a trusted path to leading universities”.
“Our collaboration with Twin will once again allow students to experience that same Bellerbys standard in the UK as part of a global network. This marks another important step in continuing our mission of opening doors for students everywhere,” Behl added.
